Simple Omelette Recipe

An Easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, of meats.

Preparation time

  • total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation 5 minutes

  • Cooking 5 minutes


Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs


Instructions

  1. Beats the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
    Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

  2. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt th
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  3. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette

  4. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold
    it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

  5. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
